## Vision
To become the intelligent layer that continuously recovers lost revenue for every online transaction by autonomously optimizing payment routing, authentication, and compliance in real time, turning decline‑driven churn into predictable growth for merchants processing $10 M+ annually.

## Value Proposition
Tap2 Optimize replaces manual, rule‑based payment‑method sequencing and static 3DS configurations with an AI‑driven recommendation engine that analyzes live transaction data, proposes optimal routing and exemption decisions, and lets merchants validate changes through instant A/B tests. The result is a self‑optimizing checkout that lifts authorization rates by up to 30 % and recovers declined revenue without requiring engineering effort or PCI‑DSS scope expansion.

## Target User
Director of Payments (or Head of E‑commerce Operations) at mid‑to‑large e‑commerce merchants with annual transaction volume between $10 M and $1 B, who are accountable for maximizing approval rates, minimizing false‑declines, and ensuring compliance with evolving SCA/PSD2 regulations.

## Key Differentiators
- **AI Recommendation Engine vs. rule‑based routing**: While competing solutions apply static routing tables or manual heuristics, Tap2 Optimize continuously generates data‑driven routing suggestions that adapt to shifting issuer behavior and recover otherwise lost transactions.
- **Integrated Real‑Time A/B Testing vs. siloed experimentation**: Traditional fraud or optimization platforms require separate testing tools and lengthy deployment cycles; Tap2 Optimize lets merchants launch unlimited test variants of payment‑method orderings and traffic splits directly within the platform, measuring uplift in real time.
- **Dynamic 3DS Exemption Automation vs. fixed exemption rules**: Competing providers apply static exemption rules that quickly become outdated; Tap2 Optimize evaluates each transaction against live regulatory profiles and issues safe exemptions on the fly, reducing friction while maintaining audit‑ready compliance.
- **Usage‑Based Billing Aligned to Recovered Revenue vs. flat SaaS fees**: Rather than charging a fixed subscription irrespective of performance, Tap2 Optimize ties a portion of its cost to the verified revenue it recovers, ensuring merchants pay only for measurable value.
- **Zero‑Touch, PCI‑DSS‑Safe Integration vs. invasive SDKs**: Many alternatives require merchants to handle tokenized card data or embed heavyweight SDKs; Tap2 Optimize connects through secure APIs that never expose raw card data, keeping the merchant’s PCI scope unchanged.

## Assumptions
- The global digital commerce market will continue to grow at >15 % CAGR, sustaining a large pool of merchants experiencing 15‑20 % baseline authorization failure rates.
- Merchants processing $10 M+ annually are willing to allocate 0.1‑0.3 % of net payment volume to optimization tools that deliver provable 10‑15 % authorization‑rate improvements.
- Regulatory frameworks governing Strong Customer Authentication (PSD2/SCA) will remain stable enough for quarterly rule‑engine updates to keep compliance automation effective.
- Sufficient historical transaction data is available (via tokenized identifiers) to train and retrain the AI recommendation engine with a confidence threshold of ≥70 % for production‑ready suggestions.
